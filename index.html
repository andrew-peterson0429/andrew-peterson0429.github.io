Who I am

Web Design and Developer in Utah

My name is Andrew Peterson, I am currently in the Utah Valley area. I love traveling, meeting new people, and exploring the world. I have a passion for all things web and technology related. This led me to pursue a degree in something technology related and now I am a recent Web Design and Development graduate from Utah Valley University looking to put my skills to the test and expand them.

My interest in technology started when I was very young. My family always enjoyed getting electronic gadgets and computers to tinker and play with. My brothers and I would always get used computer components and put them together in our own personal builds. Years later, I am continuing this tradition and building computers, but this time, I am not only putting components together, but I am also writing the software that can be used on them.

Even though I always had a fascination with technology, I also enjoyed teaching. Before being a web developer, I was an English Teacher for second language learners. I really enjoyed my experiences teaching, but after teaching around Asia for a few years, I met multiple individuals that were working while they traveled.
